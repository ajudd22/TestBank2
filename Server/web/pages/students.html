How it works

The RSC Scripture Question Bank consists of over 2500 multiple-choice questions designed to test understanding of scripture content objectively. This means no interpretation - the questions only aim to measure if you have read the scriptures carefully. While the entire bank is not availble to students to browse through, students are free to create practice tests from the bank for their needs. Just fill out the form and hit the "create" button and you'll be on your way!
