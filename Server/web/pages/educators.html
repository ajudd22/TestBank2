A streamlined, adaptable, and edifying approach to testing

The Bank is intended to act as a resource for professors who want to test effectively without the trouble of creating tests and validating them. Only BYU instructors have access to the bank in its entirety, and can browse the 2500+ items designed to objectively measure the effectiveness of students' reading of scripture.


All BYU religion professors have access to the Bank's test creation tool, which allows professors to create, diversify, and adjust their tests without sifting through large numbers of items.


The Bank is an instructor led, student informed, and student-instructor created product that intends to provide objective methods to measuring students' knowledge of scripture. Because it avoids asking about interpretation and instead focuses on close observation of the content within the scriptures itself, the bank is applicable to a diverse set of instruction methods.
